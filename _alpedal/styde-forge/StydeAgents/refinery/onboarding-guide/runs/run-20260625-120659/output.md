Week 1:  
- Tool setup: laptop, email, HRIS, Slack.  
- Meet team: 1:1 with manager, department intro.  
- Reading: company handbook, HR policies.  
- Training: HRIS basics, compliance videos.  
- First task: update employee records, schedule interviews.  
- Checkpoint: manager review Friday.  

Week 2:  
- Advanced HRIS training.  
- Shadow hiring process.  
- Draft job description.  
- Attend team meeting.  
- Review performance metrics.  

Month 1:  
- Lead interview cycle.  
- Process onboarding for new hires.  
- Analyze HR metrics report.  
- Attend cross-department meeting.  
- Checkpoint: manager review week 4.  

Months 2-3:  
- Own recruitment for junior roles.  
- Implement small process improvement.  
- Mentor new HR intern.  
- Present at team meeting.  
- Checkpoint: manager review month 3.
