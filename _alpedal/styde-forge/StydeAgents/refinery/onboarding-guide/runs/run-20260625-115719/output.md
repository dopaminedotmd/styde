# Onboarding Guide for HR Specialist

## Week 1
- Tools setup: HRIS, payroll, ATS, Slack, email
- Read: company handbook, HR policies, compliance docs
- Meet: HR team, department heads, IT support
- Training: HRIS basics, payroll intro, compliance overview
- First tasks: update employee records, schedule interviews

Checkpoint: Manager review at end of week.

## Week 2
- Training: benefits admin, onboarding process, conflict resolution
- Shadow: senior HR on employee relations, hiring process
- Tasks: draft job descriptions, assist with new hire onboarding
- Read: labor laws, benefits guides, performance review process

## Month 1
- Training: advanced HRIS, payroll processing, reporting
- Tasks: run payroll cycle, conduct exit interviews, update policies
- Project: improve one HR process (document and present)
- Meet: legal team, finance team, external HR partners

Checkpoint: Manager review at end of month.

## Months 2-3
- Training: leadership development, strategic HR planning
- Tasks: lead hiring process, handle employee relations cases
- Project: implement one HR initiative (track metrics)
- Read: industry trends, HR best practices, case studies

Checkpoint: Manager review at month 3. Performance evaluation.
