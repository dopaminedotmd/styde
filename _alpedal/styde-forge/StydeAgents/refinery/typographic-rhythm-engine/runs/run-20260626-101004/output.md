    viewport_320_to_768:
      ratio: minor_third 1.200
      reason: Compact viewports need tighter ratio transitions to preserve readable body text. A wider ratio at small sizes would push headings too large relative to available width, forcing awkward wraps. 1.200 keeps step-sizes proportional to the 320px canvas.
    viewport_768_to_1280:
      ratio: major_third 1.250
      reason: Tablet/desktop breakpoint where layout grid expands from 4-col to 8-col. Wider columns accommodate slightly larger headings. The 1.200->1.250 step-up coincides with the sidebar-to-fullwidth layout shift at 768px.
    viewport_1280_plus:
      ratio: perfect_fourth 1.333
      reason: Full-width layout at 1280px+ provides enough horizontal measure (65-75 chars) for body text at 18-21px. The bolder ratio creates explicit heading hierarchy for multi-column editorial and dashboard layouts. Also compensates for increased reading distance on large monitors.
